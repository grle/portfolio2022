SUMMARY

New England Dairy had a kickoff event for students, which happened online through Zoom. It was a fairly large event meant to teach people about dairy in an engaging and interactive way, and because it was affiliated with the NFL, it also featured football. I was tasked with creating visuals and activities, as well as storyboarding an entertaining way to teach students how a dairy farm operates.

STICKERS

I created the stickers used for the photobooth section. After participants take pictures of themselves through a photobooth program, they have the options of overlaying the stickers to the images.


STORYBOARDING

I storyboarded an activity following the adventures of Walter the Water Droplet, which shows the journey he takes throughout the dairy farm. It starts with him cooling the milk, after which he is recycled into a lagoon and used to fertilize cattle feed before being consumed by a cow and coming out as milk. Throughout the process, students learn about the temperature needed to cool the milk, the environmental cost of a gallon of milk, and the different tools that farmers use to raise cattle.
