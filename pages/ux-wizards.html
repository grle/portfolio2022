UX WIZARDS PODCAST

UX Design Podcast

SUMMARY

UX Wizards is a design collective for UX Designers to network and learn more about design. Initially founded in the South Bay in California, it has since grown to an international organization with almost 4,500 members. It has different programs and resources, one of which includes the podcast team where members interview different UX Wizards members who managed to break into the UX industry from an unrelated field.


PROJECT OVERVIEW

Challenge

The copy for the UX Wizards Podcast does not have any consistency, which makes it appear unprofessional and difficult to navigate. I took ownership of the writing styleguide to standardize all the writing that appears in the podcast.


Solution

  • Research other podcasts to understand industry standards
  • Create a writing styleguide for the podcast team
  • Rewrite the existing copy to reflect the updated styleguide

Role

  • UX Writer
  • Content Writer

Time

1 months


Tools

  • Figma
